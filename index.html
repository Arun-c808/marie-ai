Why Marie AI

Accounting is
a solved problem.

Small businesses in Malaysia spend an average of 15 hours per month on bookkeeping tasks — invoicing, data entry, bank reconciliation, tax filing preparation. Most of this is manual, repetitive, and error-prone. And yet, accurate books are fundamental to every business decision that matters.

The promise of accounting software was automation, but what most tools delivered was a slightly faster spreadsheet. You still need a human — a bookkeeper, an accountant, a finance manager — to touch every transaction. The software was the interface. The labour was still yours.

Marie AI is different. We are building a platform where the document is the input, and real-time, auditable financials are the output. No intermediate human steps. No month-end close anxiety. No chasing receipts. The accounts just work.

The Platform

From raw document
to general ledger.

Every transaction in your business begins as a document — an invoice, a receipt, a bank statement. Marie AI's pipeline transforms those documents into structured, double-entry accounting data automatically.

  • Document Ingestion Upload invoices, receipts, and bank statements by email, drag-and-drop, or API. Any format — PDF, image, CSV, XML.
  • Data Extraction OCR and AI extract structured fields — amounts, dates, vendor names, tax codes — with high accuracy, validated against source documents.
  • Transaction Normalisation Raw extractions are cleaned, deduped, and resolved into a Universal Transaction Model — a consistent schema regardless of document source or format.
  • Intent Classification Each transaction is classified by type — sale, purchase, payroll, asset acquisition, tax payment — against your accounting policy and Malaysia-specific tax rules.
  • Account Mapping Transactions are mapped to your chart of accounts using a policy engine that learns from your historical patterns and applies configurable rules.
  • Journal Generation Double-entry journal entries are created automatically — debits, credits, GST/SST allocations, foreign currency conversion — and posted to subledgers.
  • Matching & Reconciliation Bank statements are matched to ledger entries. AR and AP are cleared. Exceptions are surfaced for review. The matching engine improves with each cycle.
  • Learning Feedback Loop Every correction you make trains the system. Over time, Marie AI adapts to your business's patterns, vendors, and classification preferences.
Principles

How we think
about automation.

Documents are the source of truth. Not forms. Not spreadsheets.
Every accounting system eventually asks you to re-enter information that already exists in a document. This is wasteful and error-prone. Marie AI is built around the document as the primary input — the system reads what you already have, rather than asking you to re-key it.
Automation should be auditable. Not a black box.
We believe every automated decision in accounting must be traceable. Every journal entry Marie AI generates links back to the source document, the extraction, and the classification rule that produced it. You can follow the chain from financial statement to receipt at any time.
Compliance is built-in. Not an afterthought.
Malaysia's regulatory environment — LHDN, SST, the e-invoicing mandate — is part of the core platform, not a plugin. We maintain the compliance rules so you don't have to track regulatory changes, update templates, or worry about whether your invoices are valid.
Small businesses deserve
institutional-grade tools. Not scaled-down enterprise software.
The financial infrastructure available to large corporations — real-time management accounts, automated reconciliation, AR/AP aging, fixed asset tracking — should not require a finance department to operate. We are building these capabilities to be accessible to a business of five people running on tight margins.

Compliance

Built for
Malaysia's rules.

We built Marie AI specifically for the Malaysian regulatory environment. LHDN requirements, the e-invoicing mandate, SST filing — these are not features we added later. They are load-bearing parts of the platform.

e-Invoicing
LHDN MyInvois API
Generate, sign, and validate e-invoices through the official LHDN MyInvois API. QR code generation, XML submission, and real-time validation status — fully compliant with Malaysia's phased e-invoicing mandate.
Tax
SST Compliance
Service Tax and Sales Tax codes are mapped automatically based on transaction type and vendor category. SST-02 return data can be extracted directly from Marie AI's records at filing time.
Document Retention
7-Year Vault
All ingested documents are stored, indexed, and retrievable for a minimum of 7 years in alignment with LHDN's audit and inspection rights. Full-text search across all your financial documents — instantly.
Audit Trail
Complete Traceability
Every journal entry links to its source document, every extraction links to its source file, and every rule application is logged. If LHDN asks, you can produce the full paper trail from financial statement to original receipt in seconds.
